OpenAI acquires Software Applications Incorporated, maker of Sky

OpenAI has acquired Software Applications Incorporated, which developed Sky, a natural language interface for macOS. This acquisition aims to enhance the integration of AI into desktop experiences, making interactions with ChatGPT more intuitive and action-oriented. The move signifies OpenAI's commitment to expanding its capabilities in personal computing, potentially impacting how users engage with AI on their devices.
